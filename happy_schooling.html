Happy Schooling

Happy Schooling: Why We Love Learning Here

09 Aug 2025 • Written by: Student, Grade 12

About Akshara Vidyaashram

Akshara Vidyaashram is not only an institution where students learn about education and curriculum. It’s a place that nurtures students and inculcates habits, values, and principles within them. The sense of belonging, the peacefulness, and harmonious environment can be felt each and every day of our school journey.

Our Motive

Akshara Vidyaashram is not just a school where we read, memorize, and recite things. It’s a place where we interact, learn, and apply the concepts we read. The most important thing here in our school is learning the way we want to learn, the way where we learn things because we want to, not just because it is an established thing to do. We learn with a smile on our face, an energized mind that is hungry for knowledge, and a happy heart!

Passion that Burns Brighter

The environment of Akshara Vidyaashram is just pure bliss. It holds decades-old trees that provide shade for us, as we reconnect with nature in a world that is rushing forward with no clear destination. In Akshara, we learn that life is not a race to win and accomplish but a journey to be written by our own hands with love and passion. Our passion burns brighter where our dreams and goals aren’t mocked but held in high respect. The teachers of Akshara Vidyaashram direct us towards the goals we want to achieve and shower us with valuable insights on life. Akshara altered my thinking from ‘getting a job’ to ‘finding my passion’, which is the most important turning point in my life.

Our Identity as Aksharites

Akshara Vidyaashram is the place where they preserve the spark ignited in each of the children’s hearts. Even when society’s demands of clearing competitive exams, acing our studies, and being the perfect student weigh heavily on us, here in Akshara Vidyaashram, we learn to blossom into individuals of our own interests and identity. As we interact with each other in our class, we find more people who are like-minded, as we discuss our aspirations and motivate each other. Just like how a small stream finds its own way to the vast endless ocean, the teachers of Akshara Vidyaashram guide and motivate us towards the right path in fulfilling our aspirations.

The Sense of Belonging

In Akshara Vidyaashram, it always feels like family and a community built together with love, respect, harmony, and cooperation. We interact with the founders of our school, where they inspire us with their boundless wisdom of words. Unlike other schools, students here interact with confidence and respect with the founders. The kinship between them is remarkable, formed by mutual respect and admiration. Their words of wisdom have certainly left an impact in all of us to be striving individuals and the future pillars of our country. Akshara Vidyaashram focuses on creating students who are responsible citizens of the country.

Entrepreneurship

The other aspect of Akshara Vidyaashram is that it is a haven to stimulate the entrepreneurial mind within the students that is found to be lacking all over India due to the traditional educational system. Here we focus not just on the syllabus provided in textbooks, but beyond the syllabus. Students’ minds are molded into an entrepreneurial mindset from a young age, where they believe that they can bring changes in the world.

Life with Nature

We live in a place that preaches the importance of the ecosystem, the responsibility to protect our Mother Nature. On my birthday, I was provided with a tree as a gift from the school, symbolizing that our birth is connected to Mother Nature’s birth. Our perspective changed, from being ignorant about the changes happening around us to being responsible and taking actions. We learn to be future change-makers, to be sustainable individuals, to be the ones to protect our Mother Earth.

My Own Experience

As a student of Akshara currently studying in Class XII, I’m happy that I’m not pressurised into studying just for the sake of scoring good marks and getting a well-paid job. Akshara’s vision has changed me into believing and following my childhood dream. Being here made me feel that I could pursue my passion. It also gave me not just friends and mentors but a family that supports and motivates me to be better in my life.

Happy Schooling! The journey of being an Aksharite has changed me in many ways. The meditation, peaceful aura, and nature’s love provided by Akshara have been embedded into my spiritual soul and have molded me into a fine defining individual with a happy heart and a burning passion. More than being the person society deems me to be, I’ve found satisfaction and a clear-defining goal to achieve in my life. No matter the heights I achieve in my life, the base and the catalyst will forever be Akshara. Like the person roaming through the forest under the starry night searching for the right direction, Akshara gave us the compass needed for us to navigate through the intricate paths of life. Forever thankful to be able to be a part of the Akshara family!

Unnamalai Chetty Chavadi, Kondur, Cuddalore 607002.
