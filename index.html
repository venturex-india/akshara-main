About Us

Vision
Vision Book

Manifested in 1993 with the unwavering vision of enabling the creative forces within each student to blossom naturally and organically, Akshara Vidyaashram is a pioneer in bringing a holistic approach to education.

Just as Nature creates, nourishes, and organizes Herself to enhance the splendor of the universe, the school steadfastly upholds its mission of creating, nurturing, and grooming students to foster the magnificent development of their body, mind, and soul.

The school envisions all its students emerging as successful social leaders who will embody cheerfulness, courage, and confidence in the face of adversity, poised to confront the world and its ever-growing challenges.

"Akshara Vidyaashram intends to be the light that connects each one to their own inner light."

In 1993, our journey began as Buds & Blossoms Play School with a modest commencement, welcoming just over 20 students and three dedicated teachers. Our vision was clear - to provide "Happy Schooling" and foster the development of happy and healthy individuals. The focus was on ensuring each child experienced an unhurried childhood filled with joy and enjoyment.

In 2003, we earned affiliation with the CBSE Board up to Grade 10, leading to our rechristening as Akshara Vidyaashram. "Beyond the Syllabus" became our primary tagline, emphasizing the discovery of hidden talents and encouraging each child to pursue their innate potential. The following year witnessed our relocation to a sprawling campus, surrounded by lush greenery on the banks of River Pennar. The grounds were believed to have been graced by the Holy feet of Saint Vallalar decades ago.

In 2006, we proudly sent our first batch of students for the Grade 10 Board examinations. Concurrently, we obtained affiliation for Grades 11 and 12, marking a significant milestone in our academic journey.

In 2011, we further evolved by introducing Grade 11 with offbeat streams such as Entrepreneurship, Multimedia, Fashion Design, Agriculture, and Fine Arts, alongside conventional subjects like Computer Science, Pure Science, Biology, Zoology, Mathematics, and Commerce. This expansion reflects our commitment to providing diverse educational pathways. In 2013 the Kindergarten of Akshara Vidyaashram became Waldorf inspired.

Our Correspondent and alumnus Paveethra Ramanisankar pursues Masters in Education Technology from Harvard University to keep education at Akshara abreast with global advancements. Our then Principal Mrs.Vanaja visits Finland to understand and adapt global best practices to Akshara. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and a steadfast commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals. We look forward to the continued success and holistic development of our students in the years to come.

Traditions of Akshara

Quiet Time for Teachers: A Moment of Reflection

8:30 to 9:00 am, is a space for self-reflection for teachers that sets the tone for a harmonious and productive day of teaching and learning.

A micro gathering of parents & teachers to appreciate the goodness in each student. We believe children become what they are told they are.

Academics

Kindergarten

The Waldorf-inspired Kindergarten at Akshara has consistently been dedicated to addressing the unique learning needs of young learners, where children are immersed in a world of imitation and imagination. Central to the school's philosophy is the development of awe and wonder in young children, fostering a reverence for all living things. This foundation instills an eagerness for scholastic pursuits as they progress into primary education.

Aligned with Rudolf Steiner's philosophy, harmonizing seamlessly with Akshara's vision, we prioritize providing an unhurried childhood. Our aim is to create an environment where children find joy in learning, embracing the richness of their early years.

"Childhood and adulthood go hand in hand. When childhood is not fully lived with all its playfulness and fun, neither can adulthood be lived fully with a sense of responsibility and confidence."

Primary school

Since its inception, Akshara has been a proponent of integrated learning for primary-grade students, unfolding lessons through the interconnectedness of knowledge. The 'Beyond the Syllabus' curriculum fosters in students a profound love for learning, drawing inspiration from the world around them. This also aligns meticulously with the National Curriculum Framework (NCF). The timetable is a blend of basic academic programs and activity-based interludes, featuring gardening, field trips, cookery, reading, music, dance, yoga, sports, art, and crafts.

Students not only acquire expressive and communicative skills but also cultivate a deep appreciation for nature and imbibe values rooted in our traditions. At Akshara, values are the cornerstone of our educational philosophy, shaping individuals into good human beings and responsible citizens.

"Freedom of expression makes everyday into an opportunity to play, explore, create and feel content for who we are."

Middle School

In the dynamic Middle School phase, we imbue values and essential leadership skills, enriching academics with year-long projects, debates, and field trips. Active student involvement in school events and running parts like the honesty shop cultivates leadership, teamwork, and collaboration.

Embracing Project-Based Learning (PBL), we introduce complex problems, encouraging exploration, research, and collaborative solutions. This empowers students to own their learning, fostering curiosity and creativity, emphasizing interdisciplinary exploration and student-driven inquiry. Akshara's Middle School commits to nurturing academic excellence, character, values, and leadership potential for a successful future.

"If we want to bring up children to be leaders who know how to take accountability and responsibility, who are confident, respectful and compassionate, we have to allow them to live their true nature. A controlled individual can never lead the path of freedom; Only a free spirit can."

High School

In these units at Akshara, our approach is distinguished by meticulous planning, creating personalized academic journeys for each student. We focus on fostering individual responsibility and providing guidance to ensure students perform exceptionally well in their board exams, eliminating stress. Balanced with swimming, farming, sports, art, meditation, yoga etc students continue honing their body, mind and soul. Furthermore to support college selection and career planning, we organise college campus visits and career counseling sessions.

"We do not want the students of Akshara to ever feel that they need to give up on their passion or interests for academic results. We trust and know that they have the ability to balance it all."

The ambience embraces a cozy, beautiful setting, adorned with natural materials and gentle colors creating a soothing and visually appealing space for our young learners. Our classrooms feature a rich assortment of open-ended, natural toys and materials (no plastic), fostering imaginative and unstructured play.

Following a daily routine that includes activities like circle time, storytelling, singing, and outdoor play, our teachers aim to cultivate a sense of rhythm and predictability, providing a secure foundation for the children's holistic developmentâ€”physically, emotionally, and socially. Additionally, our Kindergarten program places a strong emphasis on spending time in nature and engaging with our school farm, aligning with the Waldorf philosophy to deepen the connection between children and the cycles of the natural world. At Akshara, we prioritize creating an ambience that respects and nurtures the unique essence of childhood, valuing play, imagination, and the establishment of a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Nestled within a distinctive hexagonal two-story building, our grade school at Akshara boasts a thoughtfully designed infrastructure that prioritizes a harmonious learning environment. The spacious and well-ventilated classrooms not only offer ample space for academic pursuits but also aim to create an inside-out connection, fostering a sense of openness and inspiration. At the heart of our academic landscape lies an open-air auditorium, a vibrant hub for various school activities, assemblies, and performances, providing a dynamic space for community engagement.

In line with our commitment to academic excellence, Akshara features state-of-the-art facilities, including dedicated physics, chemistry, biology, and computer labs. These labs are equipped with modern amenities, promoting hands-on learning experiences that enhance students' understanding and application of scientific concepts. Akshara's infrastructure reflects our dedication to providing a holistic and enriching educational experience, combining innovative architectural design with cutting-edge facilities to create a conducive environment for academic achievement, creativity, and personal growth.

Our commitment to excellence is mirrored in the availability of skilled coaches and state-of-the-art infrastructure for each activity, ensuring that students receive top-notch training and guidance.

Moreover, recognizing the importance of holistic development, we have a play gym tailored for our younger learners, providing a safe and stimulating environment for their physical growth and exploration. Through a vibrant tapestry of extracurricular activities that not only shape students for active, healthy lives beyond school but also instill values of teamwork, discipline, and passion for lifelong pursuits.

Nestled along the serene banks of the River Pennar, our 15-acre organic farm at Akshara is a vibrant hub of agricultural exploration and sustainable living. Here, children actively engage in cultivating groundnuts, traditional rice varieties, and tend to 30 native breed cows, including Gir and Kangrej, along with an endearing emu. Each child is assigned their dedicated patch, fostering a sense of responsibility and connection to nature.

The farm boasts a flourishing mango orchard, banana plantation, and provides a picturesque setting for classes held outdoors. Students actively participate in hands-on activities such as rice transplantation and crafting seed balls. Harnessing the fruits of their labor, children utilize the farm's produce to prepare delightful snacks for events, showcasing their culinary skills. From serving mint tea to grandparents on special occasions to organizing organic markets for the community, our farm is a living testament to sustainable practices and community integration, providing a holistic and enriching educational experience. Our commitment to environmental stewardship is evident in our campus, now home to 30,000 trees and over a 100 different species of passerine and aquatic birds.
